EDITORIAL Designs

Catalogue

With a focus on bold, vibrant, and elegant layout design, each page highlights the essence of every collection—Wall Art, Clay Art, Wearable Art—by artfully utilizing the judicious use of coral, chartreuse green, and deep graphite as accent colors.

Lookbook

A lookbook design with a profusion of elegance. Stylish typeface, with a bold physique, is complemented by deep graphite square shapes that gracefully adorn the edges of each page. Together, these design elements collaborate to visually narrate the story of a vibrant and colorful satin scarf collection.

periodical
